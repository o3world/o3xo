The Challenge

McKesson, a leading healthcare solutions company, recognized the transformative potential of AI but faced the common challenge of where to start. With countless potential applications across their pharmaceutical distribution operations, they needed a structured approach to identify high-impact opportunities quickly.

  • Overwhelming number of potential AI use cases across the enterprise
  • Need to align AI initiatives with strategic business objectives
  • Complex regulatory environment requiring careful consideration
  • Limited time for lengthy strategic planning processes
  • Requirement to demonstrate ROI and build executive confidence

The Solution

O3XO facilitated a rapid AI strategy workshop that brought together cross-functional teams to systematically identify, evaluate, and prioritize AI opportunities across McKesson's operations.

  • Conducted intensive collaborative workshops with stakeholders from operations, compliance, IT, and business units
  • Applied structured brainstorming techniques to identify 35+ potential AI use cases
  • Evaluated each use case using impact-effort analysis framework considering business value, technical feasibility, and resource requirements
  • Prioritized quick wins that could deliver ROI in under 90 days while building organizational AI capability
  • Created detailed implementation roadmap with phases, success metrics, and resource requirements
  • Ensured alignment with regulatory compliance and data governance requirements

The Results

In less than three weeks, McKesson gained complete clarity on their AI strategy with a concrete, prioritized roadmap that gave leadership confidence to move forward with targeted investments.

  • Rapid roadmap development in under 3 weeks compared to typical multi-month strategic planning cycles
  • 35+ AI use cases identified and evaluated across operations, customer service, compliance, and analytics
  • 5 high-impact quick wins prioritized for immediate implementation to build momentum
  • Clear phased implementation approach with resource requirements and success metrics defined
  • 100% leadership alignment on strategy and priorities
  • Confidence to move forward with targeted AI investments backed by structured analysis
"O3XO's rapid workshop process gave us exactly what we needed: clarity on where AI can drive the most value for our business, and a concrete plan to get there. In less than three weeks, we went from overwhelming possibilities to focused action."
