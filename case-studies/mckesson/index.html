The Challenge

McKesson Compile needed to rapidly develop an AI strategy that aligned with their pharmaceutical distribution business objectives. With countless potential AI applications and limited resources, they required a structured approach to identify and prioritize high-impact opportunities.

Leadership needed confidence that their AI investments would deliver tangible business value while navigating complex regulatory requirements unique to healthcare and pharmaceuticals.

Key Challenges

  • Overwhelming number of potential AI use cases
  • Need to align AI strategy with business objectives
  • Complex regulatory and compliance requirements
  • Limited time for lengthy strategic planning

The Solution

O3XO facilitated a rapid AI strategy workshop that identified, evaluated, and prioritized over 35 AI use cases, creating a clear roadmap for implementation in less than 3 weeks.

Structured Workshop Process

Intensive collaborative sessions with cross-functional teams to identify AI opportunities across operations, compliance, customer service, and data analytics.

Impact-Effort Analysis

Systematic evaluation of each use case based on business impact, technical feasibility, and implementation effort to identify quick wins and long-term opportunities.

Prioritized Roadmap

Clear, actionable implementation roadmap with phased approach, resource requirements, and success metrics for each prioritized AI initiative.

The Results

McKesson Compile gained clarity and confidence in their AI strategy, with a concrete roadmap to guide investment decisions and implementation priorities.
