The Challenge

Tyndale had decades of expert product knowledge and flame-resistant clothing insights locked away in technical documents, product catalogs, and safety guides. This valuable content wasn't effectively reaching potential customers who needed specialized protective equipment guidance.

Sales teams struggled to quickly access specific product information during customer conversations, missing opportunities to connect clients with the right solutions.

Key Obstacles

  • Expert knowledge trapped in static documents
  • Slow product information retrieval during sales
  • Missed lead generation opportunities
  • Difficulty matching customers with optimal solutions

The Solution

O3XO deployed an AI-powered knowledge platform that transformed Tyndale's product expertise into an intelligent, conversational system that both sales teams and customers could interact with naturally.

Intelligent Product Advisor

AI-driven system that understands customer needs and recommends optimal flame-resistant clothing solutions based on industry, hazard level, and compliance requirements.

Instant Knowledge Access

Sales teams and customers can quickly find specific product information, safety certifications, and application guidance through natural language queries.

Lead Capture & Nurture

Automated lead generation through intelligent product recommendations, capturing contact information from engaged prospects exploring solutions.

The Results

Tyndale's AI-powered product insight platform transformed how they connect with customers, generating consistent new leads while empowering sales teams.
