The Challenge

Tyndale, a leading provider of flame-resistant clothing and safety equipment, faced a significant challenge: years of valuable product expertise and technical knowledge were scattered across catalogs, spec sheets, and the minds of seasoned experts.

  • Product knowledge locked away in static documents and expert minds
  • Customers struggling to find specific technical information quickly
  • Sales team unable to provide instant, detailed product guidance at scale
  • Missed opportunities for lead generation through product education
  • Complex product specifications difficult to navigate for new customers

The Solution

O3XO partnered with Tyndale to build an AI-powered product insight system that transformed decades of product expertise into an intelligent, accessible resource that drives customer engagement and lead generation.

  • Developed AI system trained on Tyndale's complete product catalog and technical documentation
  • Created conversational interface allowing customers to ask natural language questions about products
  • Integrated lead capture mechanism to identify high-intent prospects seeking specific solutions
  • Implemented knowledge extraction from years of expert insights and case studies
  • Built system to provide instant, accurate answers to complex product specification queries
  • Designed seamless handoff to sales team when prospects showed buying signals

The Results

The AI-driven product insight system transformed Tyndale's customer engagement and became a powerful lead generation engine, delivering measurable business impact in under 90 days.

  • 20+ new qualified contacts generated per week through intelligent product discovery conversations
  • Years of expert product knowledge made instantly accessible to customers and sales team
  • Significant reduction in time to answer complex product questions from days to seconds
  • Higher quality leads identified through AI analysis of customer inquiries and needs
  • Sales team empowered with detailed context about prospect needs before first contact
  • Customer satisfaction improved through instant access to technical expertise
"O3XO helped us unlock decades of product expertise and turn it into a lead generation machine. We're now connecting with qualified prospects who are actively seeking our solutions, and our sales team has the insights they need to close deals faster."
