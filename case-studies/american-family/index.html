The Challenge

American Family Insurance, one of the nation's largest property and casualty insurance companies, processed thousands of complex legal documents daily. Their legal and claims teams spent hours manually reviewing contracts, medical records, police reports, and court documents to extract critical information.

  • Manual review of complex legal documents taking hours per document
  • Attorneys and adjusters spending valuable time on document review instead of strategic work
  • Risk of missing critical clauses or information in lengthy documents
  • Inconsistent extraction and interpretation of key document elements
  • Bottleneck in claims processing due to document review delays
  • High cost of manual legal document processing

The Solution

O3XO developed an AI-powered legal document intelligence system that automatically reads, understands, and extracts critical information from legal documents in seconds, providing structured summaries and highlighting key clauses for human review.

  • Built AI system trained on insurance-specific legal documents and terminology
  • Developed intelligent extraction for key elements (parties, dates, obligations, liability clauses)
  • Created natural language understanding to interpret legal language and context
  • Implemented risk flagging to highlight unusual clauses or concerning terms
  • Built structured summary generation providing document overview at a glance
  • Added comparison capabilities to identify differences between document versions
  • Integrated with claims management and legal systems for seamless workflow

The Results

The legal document intelligence system transformed American Family's document processing capabilities, dramatically accelerating claims processing while improving accuracy and allowing staff to focus on high-value work.

  • Documents processed in 30-60 seconds compared to hours of manual review
  • 85% reduction in document review time freeing attorneys and adjusters for strategic work
  • 99% accuracy in key information extraction exceeding manual review performance
  • Claims processing accelerated by 40% through faster document analysis
  • Zero critical clauses missed thanks to comprehensive AI scanning
  • Consistent, structured document summaries across all reviewers
  • Legal team satisfaction improved by eliminating tedious document reading
"O3XO's document intelligence is like having a team of legal experts working at superhuman speed. What used to take our attorneys hours now takes less than a minute, with better accuracy. It's freed our team to focus on what matters most: serving our customers and making sound decisions."
