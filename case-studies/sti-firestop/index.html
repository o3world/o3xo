The Challenge

STI Firestop, a leading provider of firestop products and solutions, faced a critical challenge: their extensive product catalog of specialized fire protection materials was difficult for customers and sales teams to navigate effectively.

  • Complex product catalog with thousands of technical specifications
  • Customers struggling to find the right products for specific applications
  • Sales team spending excessive time on product lookups during customer interactions
  • Traditional keyword search failing to understand technical requirements and use cases
  • Lost sales opportunities due to slow response times and incorrect product recommendations

The Solution

O3XO developed an AI-powered intelligent product search system that understands technical requirements, application contexts, and building codes to instantly recommend the optimal firestop solutions.

  • Built semantic search engine that understands technical terminology and application contexts
  • Trained AI on product specifications, installation guides, and building code requirements
  • Created natural language interface allowing users to describe their needs conversationally
  • Implemented intelligent filtering based on project requirements, materials, and compliance needs
  • Integrated with product database to provide real-time availability and pricing
  • Added visual product identification for field applications

The Results

The AI-powered search system transformed STI Firestop's customer experience and sales efficiency, dramatically reducing search time while improving accuracy and satisfaction.

  • Search time reduced to under 5 seconds from an average of several minutes per product lookup
  • 95% accuracy in product recommendations matching customers to optimal solutions
  • 3x increase in customer satisfaction scores for product selection experience
  • 40% reduction in sales cycle time through faster product identification
  • Significant decrease in product returns due to more accurate initial recommendations
  • Sales team productivity increased by eliminating time-consuming manual catalog searches
"O3XO's intelligent search system completely transformed how our customers and sales team find products. What used to take minutes of catalog flipping now happens in seconds with better accuracy. It's like having a product expert available 24/7."
