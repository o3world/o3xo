Opportunity

Accelerating expertise without losing precision

As a leading provider of firestop systems and engineering services, STI Firestop had built an impressive competitive advantage with their 24-hour turnaround time for Engineering Judgment (EJ) requests, far ahead of industry standards. However, with company-wide initiatives targeting even better turnarounds and growing demand for their services, their engineering team faced a bottleneck. Searching through thousands of historical EJs required manual keyword entry and extensive document review, often taking more than 15-20 minutes per search when engineers needed to find relevant precedents or similar installations. The challenge was clear: how to dramatically accelerate document retrieval while maintaining the precision and expertise that made STI's engineering services industry-leading.

Solution

Intelligent search that amplifies engineering expertise

O3XO developed a phased AI solution that seamlessly integrated with STI's existing Microsoft ecosystem and workflow. Rather than replacing their proven processes, we enhanced them with intelligent document processing and natural language search capabilities. Our approach began with ingesting a subset of STI's extensive EJ database, using advanced multimodal AI to extract detailed metadata including keywords, technical elements, relationships between components, and visual descriptions of engineering drawings. We then built a search system and embedded into their Salesforce workflow that suggests possible files when a case is created or allows engineers to describe what they needed in natural language, eliminating the guesswork of keyword combinations. The system processes queries through Azure OpenAI services, matches them against vectorized document embeddings, and returns highly relevant results ranked by similarity. Engineers can now ask a question like "Find EJs for SpecSeal in 5-ply CLT flooring", transforming document discovery from a manual hunt into an instant, intelligent retrieval process.
